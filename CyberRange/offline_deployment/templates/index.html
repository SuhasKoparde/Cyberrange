This cyber range is developed as a final year project to demonstrate practical cybersecurity skills and provide a platform for hands-on learning. The environment includes vulnerable virtual machines, realistic attack scenarios, and comprehensive monitoring capabilities.

Features:
  • Isolated virtual environments
  • Progressive difficulty levels

  • Automated scoring system
